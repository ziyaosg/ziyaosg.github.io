Ziyao Shangguan 上官子窈

I am a second-year Master's student in Computer Science at Yale University, advised by Prof. Tesca Fitzgerald and Prof. Arman Cohan. My research interest lies in interactive robot learning, particularly in enabling robots to learn from human interactions and reason over human intentions through vision. My current research focuses on utilizing multimodal LLMs to enhance robotics systems.

Prior to joining Yale, I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science & Mathematics from New York University. During my undergraduate years, I was affliated with NYU Robotic Design Team, advised by Prof. Haldun Hadimioglu.
